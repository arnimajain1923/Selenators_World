Who are We ?

Who made this fan page? is likely the first question a fan would ask. The solution to that is that I am an engineering student, so let me give you that. I made this page as an internship project since I wanted to concentrate on something interesting rather than just copying and pasting.Â 

Why Selena Gomez Fanpage ?

There is only one explanation for this and one response to that query: I adore her music and her a great deal and wanted to do something to express my affection for an artist who (like her most recent documentary film "my mind and me") inspires me in a variety of ways.
